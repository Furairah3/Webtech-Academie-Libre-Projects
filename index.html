About Our Project

Making exam preparation fairer and easier for every candidat libre

Many students in Niger prepare for national exams (BEPC and Bac) as candidats libres. Without regular classes or teachers, they often struggle to find reliable study materials and feedback.

Académie Libre gives them a simple online space where they can access past exam papers with solutions, take practice quizzes with instant grading, upload essays for automated feedback, and track their progress — all from a phone or small data plan.

Our mission: make exam preparation fairer and easier for every candidat libre.

Platform Overview

Discover how our platform works to support your learning journey

Panel 1: Login / Access Platform

Welcome to Académie Libre! Students begin by logging into their personal account using a secure email and password. Once verified, they're instantly directed to their personalized dashboard — their learning hub for all upcoming tasks, quizzes, and study materials.

Panel 2: Explore Resources

After logging in, students can easily explore a wide range of organized resources — from past exam papers to correction guides. Subjects are neatly categorized (Maths, French, History, and more), and a smart search feature helps them find exactly what they need by year, subject, or exam type.

Panel 3: Take a Quiz

Learning gets interactive with timed quizzes designed for quick self-assessment. Students select a subject, answer questions under time pressure, and receive instant scores with explanations. Each quiz helps track progress and highlights areas for improvement.

Panel 4: AI Homework Feedback

With our AI-powered feedback tool, students can upload essays or written answers and receive immediate, constructive feedback. The system scores their work, provides tips on structure, grammar, and clarity, and allows re-submissions for better results. Learning becomes a continuous, guided experience.
